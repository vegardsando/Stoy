Example Forms

The example templates below illustrate several different ways you can output your forms in your templates. The displayForm, displayField, and displayTab tags all support retaining submitted values if a form fails submission and displaying field-specific errors.

  • Contact Form (Single Line)
    craft.sproutForms.displayForm('formHandle')
  • Contact Form (Field by Field)
    craft.sproutForms.displayField('formHandle.fieldHandle')
  • Basic Fields Form (Tabs by Tab)
    craft.sproutForms.displayTab('formHandle.tabHandle')
